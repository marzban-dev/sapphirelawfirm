overview

Anil P. Gupta provides domain expertise for the Infrastructure division. He has over 39 years of global, international and Indian leadership experience in full group business and P&L management, corporate governance and as a member of various boards, having worked with different industries and regions across the world, with top global MNC’s like GE, Honeywell, ABB and Indian companies like Reliance Infrastructure, NTPC among others. In his international leadership role, he has worked for General Electric’s Energy business at their global headquarters in the USA, as their Global Market Executive in addition to various leadership roles covering Africa India, Middle East and Asia Pacific. He has worked as President & Country Head-Honeywell India overseeing all its various businesses in Aerospace, Defense and Homeland Security, Transportation, Specialty Materials & Refineries, Industrial & Automation. As the President & Head of Reliance Infrastructure he has significant experience in areas of metros, roads, regional airports & sea link which also included overseeing the first Indian PPP in metros. He has also worked in ABB and NTPC in the power sector. He has strong Project Experience in Infrastructure, Energy & Power fields across Project Life Cycle & Value Chain from concept, feasibility & detailed project reports, engineering and design, project execution, site commissioning to services and DBFOT (Design Build Finance, Operate & Transfer) and PPP Projects including contracts management and building partnership, alliances and Joint Ventures and M&A acquisition integration.
