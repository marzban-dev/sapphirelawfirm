REAL ESTATE

Overview

The Firm’s Real Estate practice has consolidated its position through work on projects across the entire real estate spectrum and a steadfast commitment to deliver effective and solution-oriented counsel. This Practice Group focuses exclusively on core conveyancing and real estate transaction support work.

With in-depth knowledge of Indian property laws, commercial drive and deep sector experience, the Firm stands out from others in the field. The Firm is able to equip its clients with the kind of solutions that align with the Government of India’s sector-specific policies governing this sector. The Firm has been involved in some of the largest and most complex transactions in the Indian real estate space.

In addition, the Firm offers a full range of legal services in real estate on all aspects of transactions including on investments, joint ventures, disposition matters, financing and restructuring of debts, foreign investments and joint ventures in various sector verticals including in mixed-use projects and in residential, commercial office, mall and warehousing and special economic zone projects, documentation and registration of commercial leases and licenses, conveyancing, property management and maintenance contracts, mortgages and other debt security documents, investigations of land title, advice on structuring real estate investments and more.
